Detective Mystery Game Guide

The Scenario: You are a Detective

You arrive at a mansion where Mr. Black has been murdered. Your goal is to solve the mystery before anyone else.

To win, you must figure out:
  • WHO committed the crime
  • WHERE it happened
  • HOW it was done

1. Setting Up the Game

  • Place all character pawns on starting squares.
  • Put weapons in different rooms.
  • Separate cards into Suspects, Rooms, and Weapons.
  • Place one of each into a secret envelope (the solution).
  • Shuffle and deal remaining cards to players.
  • Mark your cards on your Detective Pad.

2. How to Take Your Turn

A. Moving Your Pawn

  • Roll the die and move (no diagonal moves).
  • Cannot pass through occupied spaces.
  • Enter rooms to gather clues.
  • Use secret passages in corner rooms.

B. Making a Suspicion

When inside a room, you must make a suspicion:

"I suspect the crime was committed in the Lounge, by Mr. Green, with the Wrench."

  • You must name the room you're currently in.
  • Move the suspect and weapon into your room.

C. Disproving a Suspicion

  • Players check cards starting from your left.
  • If they have a matching card, they show one secretly.
  • If no one can disprove, your suspicion gains strength.
  • Update your Detective Pad accordingly.

3. Winning the Game

When confident, make an accusation:

"I accuse Colonel Mustard, in the Library, with the Candlestick."

  • If correct: You win!
  • If wrong: You are out of the game but still show cards when asked.

Important Extra Rules

  • If your character is suspected, you are moved into that room.
  • You can bluff by suspecting cards you already have.
Tip: Pay attention, take notes, and think like a detective!
